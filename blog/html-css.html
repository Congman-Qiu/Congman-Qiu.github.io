The difference between Margin, Border, and Padding

In CSS, sometimes there is confusion occurs among padding,border and margin. They are used to provide extra space or a gap. Both margin and padding targets the four sides of an element and can also work without the border property, but they differ in many ways.

    The main difference among Margin,Border and Padding
  • Padding provides the space between the border and the content of an element.
  • Margin provides the space between the border and outer elements.
  • Border provides the space between the margin and padding of an element.
